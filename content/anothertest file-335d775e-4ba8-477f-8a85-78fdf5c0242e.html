Add a part at the bottom is weird

Take a look at the "Add another part"s that come up because these are nested. I don't know why this thing is nested and therefore maybe we can ignore this, but I wanted to point it out.

You can't drag anything into the multipart.

Calculate the ball's momentum if it has a mass of 160 \text{160}~ g \text{g}.

p = m v p=mv v = 161 , 3 \rule 1 e x 0 e x km ⋅ hr − 1 v=161,3\phantom{\rule{1ex}{0ex}}\text{km}\cdot {\text{hr}}^{-1}

= 44.81 \rule 1 e x 0 e x m ⋅ s − 1 =44.81\phantom{\rule{1ex}{0ex}}\text{m}\cdot {\text{s}}^{-1}

The fastest tennis service by a man is 246,2 \text{246,2}~ km·hr^{-1} \text{km·hr^{-1}} by Andy Roddick of the United States of America during a match in London in 2004. Calculate the ball's momentum if it has a mass of 58 \text{58}~ g \text{g}.

The fastest server in the women's game is Venus Williams of the United States of America, who recorded a serve of 205 \text{205}~ km·hr^{-1} \text{km·hr^{-1}} during a match in Switzerland in 1998. Calculate the ball's momentum if it has a mass of 58 \text{58}~ g \text{g}.

We convert the mass and velocity to S.I. units.

p = m v = 0.58 kg ⋅ 56.94 m ⋅ s − 1 = 33.03 \rule 1 e x 0 e x kg ⋅ m ⋅ s − 1 p=mv=0.58\text{kg}\cdot 56.94\text{m}\cdot {\text{s}}^{-1}=33.03\phantom{\rule{1ex}{0ex}}\text{kg}\cdot \text{m}\cdot {\text{s}}^{-1}

If you had a choice of facing Shoaib, Andy or Venus and didn't want to get hurt, who would you choose based on the momentum of each ball.

The ball with the smallest momentum gives you the least chance of being hurt and so you would choose to face Venus.
